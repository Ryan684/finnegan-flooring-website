01 — Services

Four screed systems, one specialist crew.

We specify, pump and finish the right screed for the build programme — pumped or hand-laid, traditional or rapid, bonded or floating.

S/01Pumped

Pumped traditional sand/cement screed

The bread-and-butter system for large floor areas. Pumped on-site for speed, consistency and access to tight or upper-floor pours.

TypeTraditional
S/02Hand-laid

Ready-mixed traditional sand/cement screed

Site-delivered ready-mixed screed for measured areas and detail work where pumped delivery isn't required.

TypeTraditional
S/03Flowing

Anhydrite screed

Self-levelling calcium-sulphate screed — laid thinner, faster and flatter than traditional. Ideal over underfloor heating.

TypeCalcium-Sulphate
S/04Rapid

Fast drying & rapid setting screed

For programmes where every day on the critical path counts. Foot-traffic and floor finishes in a fraction of the time.

TypeRapid Cure

02 — About

Founded in 1997.
Reputation built pour by pour.

The Company

Finnegan Flooring Limited was founded in 1997 by Managing Director Andy Finnegan. As one of the south coast's leading floor screeding contractors, we've acquired a high-standard reputation through expertise and experience that have led to a constantly growing client base.

Andy has a reliable management team in place and an experienced site team capable of working to tight schedules without affecting the final product. Finnegan Flooring Limited has a labour force of skilled tradesmen — all CSCS card holders. We will carry out work on any project, from a small extension with a local builder to a brand-new school with a nationally recognised construction firm.
